About Me

My name is George Raymond and I am a full stack web developer currently located in Los Angeles, California. As a recent graduate of UCLA’s full stack development boot camp, I am learning and expanding my skill set on a daily basis. I am fascinated and passionate about technology and enjoy the endless supply of new material there is to explore in this field. Through my experience with the coding boot camp at UCLA I had the opportunity to personally build and oversee the development of multiple full stack web applications. I am extremely driven and self-sufficient, If I do not know how to do something I will go to great length to teach myself what I am looking for. When it comes to coding I have learned the answer is always there, you just have to know how to look for it. I have worked on both the front and backend of projects I have been involved with extensively. At this point in my career I am proficient and/or have direct experience with following technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, React, Node.js, REST, NPM, Yarn, Express, API’s, Handlebars.js, MySQL, MongoDB and Firebase. Outside of tech, I am a top tier foodie and coffee enthusiast. I love exploring and traveling for amazing food and experiences. I live in Hollywood with my Girlfriend Geneva and our furry son Smoke.
